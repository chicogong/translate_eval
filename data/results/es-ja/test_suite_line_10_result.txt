(Machine learning model wa, user-generated content no kanjo bunseki o jikko suru tame ni, seisen sareta dataset de training saremashita.)

Explanation:
1. "Modelo de aprendizaje automático" → 機械学習モデル (standard technical term)
2. Used polite form "されました" for formal context
3. Restructured to Japanese SOV pattern: Subject (モデル) → Object (感情分析) → Verb (トレーニングされました)
4. No cultural adaptation needed as the technical concept is universal
